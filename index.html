Prayer Times

“Prayer is the Pillar of the Religion.”

Salah:

  • Fajr : Dawn, before sunrise.
  • Zhuhr : Midday, after the sun passes its highest.
  • Asr : The late part of the afternoon.
  • Maghrib : Just after sunset.
  • Isha'a : Between sunset and midnight.
